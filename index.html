Capabilities

Every layer of human emotion, decoded

Edge-Powered AI Engine
Our dedicated desktop software runs powerful deep-learning models locally on your Mini PC, ensuring high-speed facial analysis without relying on expensive cloud processing.
Crowd & Multi-Face Tracking
Simultaneously detects and analyzes up to 15 distinct faces in a single camera frame. Perfect for tracking group engagement in retail aisles, exhibition booths, and queues.
Live Dashboard Synchronization
While heavy video processing happens locally, anonymous emotion metrics are instantly synced to your secure cloud database, allowing you to monitor live insights from any device.
100% Anonymous & Private
Privacy by design. The local software processes video frames in memory and instantly discards them. Only anonymous emotion tallies are stored—no photos or personal data are ever saved.

How it works

From hardware setup to real-time insights

01
Hardware Setup
Mount the HD camera in your target engagement zone and connect it directly to your local mini PC or processing unit.
02
Install Application
Run our lightweight installer to securely deploy the emotion detection engine into your dedicated local application directory.
03
Initialize Event
Launch the desktop software with your license key. The system instantly begins tracking multiple faces, storing the data in a unified cloud structure.
04
Analyze Dashboard
Log into the web portal from any device to view live emotional metrics, track audience engagement trends, and export business insights.
